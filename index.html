When to introduce retainers

Don't lead with retainers — lead with the project packages. Once they've experienced working with you through Tier 1 or 2, introduce the retainer conversation naturally at the first review point. Trust has to come first.

The value argument for Aluzie

They're a small technical team. Writing LinkedIn posts, updating messaging, and reviewing documents in-house will always fall to the bottom of the pile. A retainer solves that problem permanently — and costs less than a single freelance hire.

How to present this

Lead with Tier 2 as the project recommendation. Frame Tier 1 as the floor and Tier 3 as the growth path. Introduce retainers at the kick-off or first delivery review — never in the opening proposal. Let the work earn the conversation.
